Our Heritage

A Legacy of Roots & Community

Founded in 1972 on the shores of Lake Washington, Windermere Real Estate has grown from a single office into the Pacific Northwest's most recognized real estate brand.

1972

Community-Driven.

What began with John W. Brenneman's vision of a real estate company that genuinely cares about its neighbors has become a tradition spanning over five decades. Our agents don't just sell homes — they build communities.

Every transaction is guided by our core values: integrity, service, and an unwavering commitment to the Pacific Northwest way of life. We believe in giving back, showing up, and making every neighborhood a little better than we found it.

How It Works

Your Journey Home

A guided path from first conversation to closing day, with expert support every step of the way.

Step 1

Discovery Consultation

We sit down together — ideally with a cup of locally roasted coffee — to understand your needs, dreams, and timeline for finding the perfect Pacific Northwest home.

Step 2

Market Analysis & Strategy

Your agent provides a comprehensive market analysis, customizing a strategy that positions you competitively whether buying or selling in today's market.

Step 3

Active Search or Listing

For buyers: curated property tours across neighborhoods. For sellers: professional staging, photography, and marketing that showcases your home's unique character.

Step 4

Negotiation & Closing

Expert negotiation ensures the best outcome. We handle inspections, paperwork, and all details — so you can focus on the excitement of your new chapter.

Step 5

Welcome Home

Keys in hand, you're part of the Windermere family. Our relationship continues with neighborhood resources, maintenance tips, and lifelong support.
