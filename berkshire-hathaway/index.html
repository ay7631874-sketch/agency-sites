Our Heritage

A Tradition of Excellence

Founded in 2013 in the heart of Omaha, Nebraska, Berkshire Hathaway HomeServices has grown from a visionary dream into one of the most respected names in real estate. Our heritage is rooted in the timeless values of integrity, trust, and an unwavering commitment to our clients' aspirations.

With over 50,000 distinguished agents across 1,500+ offices, we represent not merely transactions, but the fulfillment of legacies. Each home we represent carries the weight of our reputation—a reputation built over decades of service to families seeking the extraordinary.
