Our Heritage

The Hanna Family Timeline

Three generations of building trust, one family at a time.

1957

The Beginning

Howard W. Hanna Jr. opens the first Howard Hanna Real Estate office in Cleveland, Ohio, establishing a legacy built on trust and personal service.

1975

Regional Expansion

Second generation joins the business. Howard Hanna expands across Ohio and Pennsylvania, becoming a dominant force in Northeast real estate.

1997

Mortgage & Insurance

Howard Hanna Financial Services launches, offering one-stop shopping with mortgage, insurance, and title services under one trusted name.

2001

National Reach

The third generation of Hanna leadership takes the helm. The company expands to 13 states with over 7,000 agents serving families nationwide.

2012

Real Living Partnership

Howard Hanna acquires Real Living, creating one of the largest residential real estate companies in America with a coast-to-coast presence.

Today

11,000+ Agents Strong

With 500+ offices and $37B+ in annual sales, Howard Hanna remains proudly family-owned — serving families across the nation with the same values.
