About Us

A Legacy of Celebrations

Prema Kunj Resort is a marriage lawn and resort on Unnao Balaji Road in Goramachhiya, Jhansi — a venue designed for the kind of weddings that stay in memory. Expansive lawns for open-air ceremonies, banquet halls for intimate gatherings, and comfortable resort rooms for guests travelling from out of town.

The space is shaped by years of hosting celebrations — every corner considered, every detail in place. We host weddings, receptions, mehendi nights, and family gatherings.
