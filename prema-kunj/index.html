Welcome to Prema Kunj

Nestled on Unnao Balaji Road in Goramachhiya, Jhansi, Prema Kunj Resort is one of the most sought-after marriage lawns and resort venues in the Bundelkhand region. Our sprawling property offers the perfect blend of elegance, comfort, and modern amenities for your special celebrations.

From grand wedding ceremonies and receptions to intimate gatherings and corporate events, we create unforgettable experiences with meticulous attention to detail. Our dedicated team ensures every moment of your celebration is nothing short of magical.
