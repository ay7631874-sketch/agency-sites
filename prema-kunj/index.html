Where celebrations find their perfect setting — lawns, halls, and quiet rooms under the Jhansi sky.

Prema Kunj Resort is a marriage lawn and resort on Unnao Balaji Road in Goramachhiya, Jhansi — a venue designed for the kind of weddings that stay in memory. Expansive lawns for open-air ceremonies, banquet halls for intimate gatherings, and comfortable resort rooms for guests travelling from out of town. The space is shaped by years of hosting celebrations — every corner considered, every detail in place.

We host weddings, receptions, mehendi nights, and family gatherings. Call ahead for availability on weekends and during the wedding season.
