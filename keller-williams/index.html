ABOUT US

BUILT ON
BELIEF

Keller Williams Realty was founded in 1983 by Gary Keller and Joe Williams in Austin, Texas. What started as a single office has grown into the world's largest real estate franchise, fueled by a technology-driven model that puts agents first.

"We don't just sell homes. We build communities, empower entrepreneurs, and create legacies."

— Gary Keller, Co-Founder

With over 180,000 associates across 1,000+ offices worldwide, KW combines cutting-edge technology with unparalleled training, education, and profit-sharing opportunities that transform agents into business owners.
