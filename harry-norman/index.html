Our Heritage

A Legacy of Southern Elegance Since 1930

Harry Norman, Realtors was founded in 1930 by Harry Norman Sr., one of Atlanta's most respected business leaders. From our earliest days in the heart of Buckhead, we have been synonymous with integrity, discretion, and an unwavering commitment to our clients.

As Atlanta's oldest residential real estate company, we have witnessed the city's remarkable transformation while remaining rooted in the values that have defined us for generations. Our agents are not just professionals — they are stewards of Atlanta's finest neighborhoods.
