Our Heritage on the
East Coast

Founded in 1968 by Hank Lee and P. Wesley Foster Jr. in Fairfax, Virginia, Long & Foster Real Estate has grown from a single office into the largest privately owned real estate company in the United States.

Rooted in the tradition and values of the Mid-Atlantic region, we've built our reputation on trust, integrity, and an unwavering commitment to serving our communities. For over five decades, families across the East Coast have turned to Long & Foster to guide them home.

Today, with more than 200 locations across nine states and the District of Columbia, our network of affiliated companies provides a full range of real estate, mortgage, title, and insurance services.

1968

Founded in Fairfax, VA

Hank Lee and P. Wesley Foster Jr. open the first Long & Foster office, bringing a new standard of service to Northern Virginia real estate.

1980s

Regional Expansion

The company expands throughout Virginia, Maryland, and Washington D.C., becoming the dominant name in Mid-Atlantic real estate.

1990s

Full-Service Network

Long & Foster launches affiliated companies in mortgage, title, and insurance, offering clients a seamless home-buying experience.

2007

#1 in the Nation

Long & Foster becomes the largest privately owned residential real estate company in the United States by sales volume.

Today

200+ Locations

With over 200 offices across nine states, Long & Foster continues to serve hundreds of thousands of families every year.
