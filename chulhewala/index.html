Our Story

The Taste of Tradition, Served Fresh

At Chulhewala, we bring the authentic flavors of North Indian kitchens to your plate. Our name says it all — food cooked on a chulha (traditional clay stove), just the way it's been done for generations.

Located in the heart of Jhansi at Pal Colony, Gwalior Road, we've been serving the community with love, warmth, and dishes that taste like home. Every spice is handpicked, every recipe perfected over years.
