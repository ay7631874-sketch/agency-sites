Chulhewala is an independent family kitchen on Gwalior Road — a place where the butter chicken is reduced by hand, the lal maas is tempered with whole spices, and the room is warmed by live music most evenings. We cook the dishes we grew up eating: rich, slow, and unapologetically seasoned. No shortcuts. No presets.

Most nights you will find us full. Call ahead if you are coming on a weekend.
