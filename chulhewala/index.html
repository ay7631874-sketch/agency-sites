Our Story

A family kitchen, open to everyone

Chulhewala is an independent family kitchen on Gwalior Road — a place where the butter chicken is reduced by hand, the lal maas is tempered with whole spices, and the room is warmed by laughter and live music most evenings.

We cook the dishes we grew up eating: rich, slow, and unapologetically seasoned. No shortcuts. No presets. Just honest food served the way it should be.
