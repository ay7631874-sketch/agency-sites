A Legacy of Trust

Since the dawn of the twentieth century

Founded in the year nineteen hundred and six in the borough of Madison, New Jersey, Coldwell Banker has stood as a pillar of integrity in the American real estate landscape. For more than a century, our name has been synonymous with trust, discretion, and an unwavering commitment to the families and communities we serve. We were born during an era when a handshake carried the weight of law, and that principle of personal honor continues to guide every transaction we undertake.

Today, with a network spanning more than one hundred thousand agents across three thousand offices throughout the globe, we remain steadfast in our founding mission: to provide extraordinary service to every client, in every market, at every price point. Our agents are not mere intermediaries — they are trusted advisors, community leaders, and stewards of the most significant financial decisions their clients will ever make. Whether one seeks a modest cottage or a grand estate, the Coldwell Banker promise endures.
