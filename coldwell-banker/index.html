Our Legacy of Excellence in Real Estate

Est. 1906

A Century of Trust

Founded by Colbert Coldwell and later joined by Benjamin Banker, Coldwell Banker has grown from a single San Francisco office to a global real estate powerhouse. For over 118 years, our commitment to integrity, service, and excellence has remained unwavering. Today, we operate in over 40 countries with more than 100,000 affiliated agents — each one dedicated to finding you not just a house, but a home.

Global Reach

Market Leadership

Coldwell Banker Real Estate LLC is a subsidiary of Anywhere Real Estate Inc., one of the largest real estate services companies in the United States. Our affiliated agents are trusted advisors who combine deep local knowledge with access to powerful global resources. With $236+ billion in annual sales volume, we don't just participate in the luxury market — we define it.

Luxury Division

Coldwell Banker Global Luxury

The Global Luxury program represents the pinnacle of Coldwell Banker's offerings.
