Explore Miami

Prestigious Beach Communities

Discover the unique character of each Miami neighborhood, from historic Coral Gables to the glamorous shores of Key Biscayne.

🌴

Coral Gables

The "City Beautiful" — Mediterranean Revival architecture, the Biltmore Hotel, and tree-lined boulevards make this one of America's most desirable communities.

🏖️

Key Biscayne

An island paradise just minutes from downtown Miami with pristine beaches, Bill Baggs Park, and some of the most exclusive waterfront estates in Florida.

🏙️

Brickell

Miami's financial district and urban core — luxury high-rises, world-class restaurants, and a cosmopolitan lifestyle steps from Biscayne Bay.

🎨

Wynwood

The arts district of Miami — street murals, galleries, craft breweries, and the iconic Wynwood Walls create a vibrant, creative atmosphere.

🌿

Pinecrest

An affluent village known for its lush canopy, top-rated schools, and sprawling estate homes on manicured acre-plus lots.

🌅

Coconut Grove

Miami's oldest neighborhood — bohemian charm, waterfront parks, the CocoWalk, and a tree-canopied streetscape unlike anywhere else.
