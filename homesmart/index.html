🌿 About HomeSmart

Real Estate, Reimagined

Founded in 2000 in Scottsdale, Arizona, HomeSmart disrupted the traditional real estate model by introducing a flat-fee commission structure. We believe every agent deserves to keep more of what they earn, and every client deserves a transparent, modern experience.

With over 20,000 agents and 350+ offices across the country, we combine cutting-edge technology with a human-first approach to make buying and selling homes effortless.
